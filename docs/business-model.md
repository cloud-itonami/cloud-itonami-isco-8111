# Business Model: Mine/Quarry-Site Scheduling & Logistics Coordination Service

## Classification

- Repository: `cloud-itonami-isco-8111`
- ISCO-08: `8111`
- Occupation: Miners and Quarriers
- Social impact: mine-safety, worker-safety, public-safety

## Scope

**This actor coordinates mine/quarry-site scheduling and logistics
only.** It never performs mining/quarrying work itself, never
finalizes a mine/quarry-operation-execution decision (authorizing
extraction/excavation to proceed), and never overrides a
mine-safety-officer's or site-supervisor's judgment. Miners and
quarriers work underground or in open-pit quarry sites — cave-in/
collapse, gas exposure, heavy-equipment and fall hazards can cause
death or serious injury, categorically higher-stakes than ordinary
workshop trades — so every proposal this actor's advisor can make is
limited to coordination, not execution and not authorization.

## Customer

- mining and quarrying operators
- mine/quarry-site crew leads and shift supervisors

## Offer

- work-record logging (extraction-log, progress notes, materials usage)
- crew/shift-schedule scheduling proposals
- safety-concern surfacing (ground-stability, gas-detection, equipment-condition)
- mining-equipment/consumables supply-order coordination

## Revenue

- monthly coordination-platform retainer
- per-site/per-crew logistics fee

## Trust Controls

- no mine/quarry-operation-execution decision (authorizing extraction/
  excavation to proceed) is ever finalized by this actor
- no mine-safety-officer's or site-supervisor's judgment is ever
  overridden by this actor
- every safety-concern flag ALWAYS escalates to human sign-off, no
  exceptions, ever
- supply orders above the registered cost threshold always escalate to
  human sign-off
- mine/quarry site and miner provenance is independently verified
  before any coordination action
- coordination and audit records are auditable, not editable
